get inspired.

  • unsplash

    even just scrolling around and looking at the pictures here can inspire a cool idea, but this is also just..the absolute best resource i've ever come across for beautiful, high resolution free (!!!) images to use in projects.

  • awwwards

    whenever i want to start on a project, but i have no ideas..this is the place i go to first. an amazing resource for inspiration and ideas, and a great place to see how designers are solving visual problems that you may be working on yourself

  • animate.css

    i use this css library in..virually everything now. it's flexible and customizable, but also super easy to implement. the perfect balance. it can be used right in your HTML, modified and implemented in your CSS, or even used in javascript/jQuery functions for some really cool customizations as well.

  • youtube

    you may think this is silly, but anytime i have a complicated coding question i check youtube first. there's almost always someone who has a full tutorial showing the process from start to finish, and they alsi usually have a link to a snippet of code you can borrow in the description.

  • stackoverflow

    the go to place for anyone who writes code to ask and answer questions. a vibrant community of programmers have probably answered any question you might have.

  • w3schools

    i come here for the basics. like when my brain is completely fried and i forget how to make a link (yes, i have literally forgotten how to write a simple anchor tag..sometimes its the dumb basic stuff that will take you out!)
